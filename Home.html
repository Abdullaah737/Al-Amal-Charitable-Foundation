About Us

We, the members of AL-AMAL CHARITABLE FOUNDATION a not for-profit and non-political organisation do firmly and solemnly resolve to provide for ourselves o constitution and to be governed by the provisionstherein contained.

  • TO PROMOTE THE WELFARE OF VULNERABLE PEOPLE IN MUSLIM COMMUNITIES.
  • TO PROMOTE CHARITABLE COURSES FOR HUMANITY.
  • TO PROVIDE FREE SUPPORT ON COMMUUNITY DEVELOPMENT, FREE WATER, FREE MEDICAL SERVICES AND OTHER SUSTINABLE PROJECTS.
  • TO PROVIDE MONETARY AND HUMANITARIAN SUPPORT AT ALL TIMES FOR VULNERABLE PEOPLE.
  • TO PROVIDE FREE ASSISTANCE ON ALL DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS FOR MUSLIM COMMUNITIES.
  • TO PROMOTE ANY OTHER PROJECTS INCIDENTAL TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF HUMANITY.
